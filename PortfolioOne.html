About Me

Hello! My name is Hlanganani Hlongwane, and my journey into the world of technology began in high school, where I developed a strong interest in both technical and mechanical fields. After high school, I gained 1 year of hands-on experience as a CNC programmer, operator, and setter. This role allowed me to sharpen my precision, problem-solving, and technical skills in a manufacturing environment.

Following my time as a CNC programmer, I pursued a 3-year diploma in Software Development, which deepened my understanding of coding, application design, and system architecture. Over the course of my studies, I became proficient in languages such as JavaScript (React, Node.js), Java (Spring), Kotlin (Ktor), PHP, and C# (ASP.NET). This exposure sparked my passion for building scalable and efficient applications, both on the front-end and back-end.

In addition to programming, I have developed strong skills in database management using SQL, particularly with MySQL and PostgreSQL, and an understanding of DevOps practices using tools like Git for seamless integration, delivery, and deployment of applications. I thrive on solving complex problems through code and aim to write clean, maintainable code that enhances user experience and drives business outcomes.

As a dedicated software developer, I enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams to bring creative ideas to life, whether it's designing intuitive user interfaces or optimizing backend performance. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and continuously push the boundaries of what technology can achieve.
